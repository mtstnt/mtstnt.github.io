This project explores how a portfolio can feel editorial without becoming heavy.

## What I built

- A unified homepage for profile, projects, and recent writing
- Markdown-driven content pipelines for posts and project entries
- Theme tokens and dark mode behavior shared across layouts

## Why it matters

The goal was to make it easy to publish consistently while preserving a distinct visual identity.
